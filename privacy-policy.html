Privacy Policy for Tabeq

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Tabeq ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for Tabeq, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Apartment/building information (floor number, apartment number)

1.2 Building and Payment Information

  • Building details (name, address, location coordinates)
  • Expense and bill information
  • Payment records and status
  • Building balance information

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Location data (only when using the map feature to register a building)
  • Push notification tokens (for sending notifications)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Tabeq service
  • Manage building expenses and payments
  • Enable communication between building administrators and residents
  • Send notifications about bills, payments, and building updates
  • Verify user identity and prevent fraud
  • Improve and optimize our app
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase services to store and protect your data:

  • Firebase Authentication: Securely manages user accounts
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores building, user, and payment data
  • Firebase Storage: Stores profile pictures and uploaded documents
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivers push notifications

Your data is stored on secure servers in Europe (Belgium - europe-west1) and is protected using industry-standard encryption.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Within Your Building

  • Building administrators can view resident information (name, apartment number, payment status)
  • Your name and apartment number are visible to your building administrator
  • Payment information is only visible to you and your building administrator

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For hosting, database, authentication, and storage
  • Google Maps: For location selection when registering buildings

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or security issues

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information in the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Children's Privacy

Tabeq is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide our services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

8. Location Data

We collect location data only when:

  • Building administrators use the map feature to register their building location
  • You grant location permission in your device settings

Location data is not collected continuously and is only used for building registration purposes. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Tabeq does not use cookies. We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns, but this data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending a notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of Tabeq after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Firebase servers located in Europe (Belgium). If you are located outside the European Economic Area, your data may be transferred to and processed in Europe. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
