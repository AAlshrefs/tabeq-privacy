🗑️ Delete Your Tabeq Account

We're sorry to see you go! This page explains how to request deletion of your Tabeq account and associated data.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

1 Open the Tabeq app

Launch the Tabeq application on your mobile device and log in to your account.

2 Go to Settings

Navigate to the Settings screen (tap the gear icon ⚙️ in your dashboard or profile).

3 Scroll to "Delete Account"

Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings page and tap on "Delete Account" button.

4 Confirm deletion

Read the warning message carefully and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

ℹ️ Alternative Method: If you cannot access the app or prefer email, you can send a deletion request to our support email below.

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot delete your account through the app, send an email to:

📧 Email: support@tabeq.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your Tabeq account
  • Phone number registered in the app
  • Building name (if you remember it)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
Send Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

Data Type Action
Personal Information (name, email, phone) ✅ Deleted
Profile Picture ✅ Deleted
Apartment/Building Association ✅ Deleted
Payment Records (personal view) ✅ Deleted
Account Authentication Data ✅ Deleted
App Preferences and Settings ✅ Deleted
Push Notification Tokens ✅ Deleted

What Data May Be Retained?

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or operational purposes:

Data Type Retention Reason
Financial Transaction Records (anonymized) Up to 7 years Legal/tax compliance
Building Payment History (anonymized) Until building deleted Building administrator records
Aggregated Analytics Data (anonymized) Indefinitely App improvement (no personal identifiers)
Backup Logs (anonymized) 30 days System recovery
Note: Retained data will be fully anonymized and will not contain any personally identifiable information (PII) that can be linked back to you.

Special Cases

If You Are a Building Administrator

Before deleting your account:

  • Transfer building ownership to another administrator, OR
  • Delete the building first (this will remove all residents)

If you delete your account while still being the only administrator, residents will lose access to building data.

If You Have Outstanding Payments

You can still delete your account, but:

  • Payment records will be retained (anonymized) for the building administrator
  • Outstanding bills will remain visible to the administrator
  • You should settle any outstanding payments before deletion

Timeline

Action Timeframe
Account deletion request submitted Immediately
Account deactivated (cannot log in) Within 24 hours
Personal data deleted from active systems Within 7 days
Data removed from backups Within 30 days
Email confirmation sent After completion (7-30 days)

After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • ❌ You cannot log in to Tabeq
  • ❌ You cannot recover your account or data
  • ❌ Your name will be removed from building resident lists
  • ❌ You will not receive any notifications
  • ✅ You can create a new account with the same email (fresh start)

Your Rights

Under data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email above.
